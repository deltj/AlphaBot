This is a **Stamina Damage-Dealer** that can be either a **Necromancer** or a **Warden**. The emphasis is on burst damage; for the set combination, please contact @AGSNATURE directly.

The primary ultimate is **Dawnbreaker** (the higher-damage morph):
https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/dawnbreaker-of-smiting

The second ultimate depends on the class; for a *Necromancer* it is **Pestilent Colossus**.
https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/pestilent-colossus

The required skills include class-based AOE damage and the following

1) *Spin to win* that is used as an execute.
https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/whirling-blades

2) **Bone Shield** (either morph) to be kept active at all times during combat.
https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/bone-shield

3) **Echoing Vigor** to be used defensively when the group is taking heavy damage.
https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/echoing-vigor

4) On a Warden, keeping up **Expansive Frost Cloak** on the whole group is primordial.
https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/expansive-frost-cloak
