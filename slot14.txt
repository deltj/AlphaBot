This is **Magicka damage-dealer** that can be either a **Dragonknight**, a **Warden**, or a **Nightblade**.
<https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Special:EsoBuildData?id=265287>
One of the two-piece sets is **Vicious Death** which is a light-armor set (bought with AP in Cyrodiil) and the other is **Clever Alchemist** which is craftable. The monster set is **Balorgh** (vet DLC dungeon or golden vendor) and the build also requires the **Malacath ring** (requires the Greymoor chapter).
<https://eso-sets.com/set/vicious-death>
<https://eso-sets.com/set/clever-alchemist>
<https://eso-sets.com/set/balorgh>
<https://eso-sets.com/set/malacaths-band-of-brutality>
The three jewelry pieces need to be traited as *Harmony*. In all cases, the primary ultimate is **Eye**.
<https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/eye-of-the-storm>
For a DK, the second ultimate should be **Shifting Standard**.
<https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/shifting-standard>
The main tasks include AOE damage with destro staff skills and class abilities together with
1) **Radiating Regeneration** on the backbar *Restoration staff* to cast frequently before and between pushes.
<https://eso-skillbook.com/skill/radiating-regeneration>
